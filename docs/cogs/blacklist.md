# Blacklist

## `/blacklist show`

> Shows the blacklist.     
> **Usage:** ``User with `moderate_members` permissions``   
> **Examples:**     
> `/blacklist show`

## `/blacklist add`

> Add a member to the blacklist. 
> **Usage:** ``User with `moderate_members` permissions``   
> **Examples:**     
> `/blacklist add <role>`

## `/blacklist remove`

> Remove a member from the blacklist.    
> **Usage:** ``User with `moderate_members` permissions``   
> **Examples:**     
> `/blacklist remove <role>`

## `/blacklist clear`

> Clear the blacklist.  
> **Usage:** ``User with `moderate_members` permissions``   
> **Examples:**     
> `/blacklist clear`
